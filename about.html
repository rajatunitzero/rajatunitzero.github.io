Studio Unit Zero is a collaborative architectural design initiative founded by three designers with a shared interest in architectural exploration, research, and conceptual practice. The studio functions as an independent platform for developing early access and exploratory projects, allowing for focused investigations into spatial systems, material relationships, and architectural ideas. Our work emphasizes process-driven design, where drawings, models, and visual studies form the basis of architectural inquiry. Studio Unit Zero operates alongside professional practice, serving as a space for experimentation and collaboration outside of employment-based work. All projects presented on this website are independently produced by the studio and do not represent the work, views, or intellectual property of any firm or organization the members are/were professionally associated with. Through this platform, the studio seeks to engage in catering to design and share a body of work that reflects our collective approach to architecture - rooted in curiosity, rigor, and thoughtful design.
