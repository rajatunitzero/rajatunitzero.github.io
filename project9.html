The dynamic diagonal

Pune

Project Type

Retail

Built Area

700 sft.

Commercial design requires a perfect synthesis of brand identity and operational flow. For this bakery, we translated the brand’s vibrant energy into the physical space using bold blue fluted paneling and dynamic diagonal stripes. Our process focused on lighting strategy and counter placement to ensure the products remain the hero, creating an inviting retail experience that balances playful aesthetics with service efficiency
